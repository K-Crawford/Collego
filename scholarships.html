    • St. Louis Scholarships

    Since the number of students who qualify is limited based on location, the chances of winning local scholarships are often greater than the odds of winning national scholarships. This can make local scholarships an important option to consider for funding your education.


    ISL Midwest Senior Scholarship

    Open to high school seniors who are planning to attend an eligible college, physically or virtually,in the fall.

    $1,000

    Kris Paper Legacy Scholarship For Women In Technology

    Awarded to a graduating female high school senior seeking a degree in a technology-related field.

    $1,500

    St. Louis Mothers Mary Joan Wolf Memorial Scholarship

    Offers scholarships for students seeking enrollment in post-high school education.

    $1,000
